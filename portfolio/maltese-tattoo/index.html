Maltese Tattoo

Maltese Tattoo

Malta

Just 3.5km offshore of Malta is a beautiful pattern of lobes and ripples of sand overlaying maerl with sand and gravel.  This is backscatter imagery from a multibeam bathymetry system.  The dark areas show where the sonar signal has been absorbed by the maerl whereas the ripples of sand give strong backscatter and thus a lighter colour grey. These curls and swirls are not revealed by the bathymetry in this area.

The individual patterns are very reminiscent of the kind of henna tattoos encircling arms or ankles. Whether you can see ghosts, lions, dragons or something else depends on your imagination.
